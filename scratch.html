7 Wonders of the World:

  1. Great Wall of China
    • Location: China
    • An ancient series of walls and fortifications, totaling more than 13,000 miles in length, built to protect against invasions and raids.
  2. Petra
    • Location: Jordan
    • A historical and archaeological city famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system. Also known as the "Rose City" due to the color of the stone.
  3. Christ the Redeemer
    • Location: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
    • An iconic statue of Jesus Christ standing 98 feet tall atop the Corcovado Mountain, offering panoramic views of the city.
  4. Machu Picchu
    • Location: Peru
    • An ancient Incan city located in the Andes Mountains, renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone construction and panoramic mountain views.
  5. Chichen Itza
    • Location: Yucatan, Mexico
    • A large pre-Columbian archaeological site built by the Maya civilization, known for the pyramid of El Castillo.
  6. Roman Colosseum
    • Location: Rome, Italy
    • An ancient amphitheater used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles, capable of holding up to 80,000 spectators.
  7. Taj Mahal
    • Location: Agra, India
    • A white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, celebrated for its stunning architecture and gardens.
